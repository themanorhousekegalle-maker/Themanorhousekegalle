Our Story

A Heritage Retreat

Nestled in the lush greenery, The Manor House is a lovingly restored colonial-era mansion that offers a serene escape. Surrounded by 10 acres of tropical gardens with fruit trees, coconut, tea, cinnamon and banana, we blend old-world charm with contemporary amenities.

Our family-run hotel provides personalise service, Sri Lankan hospitality and a peaceful atmosphere perfect for relaxation.
