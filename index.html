Our Story

A Heritage Retreat

Nestled in the lush greenery of Kegalle, The Manor House is a lovingly restored colonial-era bungalow that offers a serene escape. Surrounded by tropical gardens, we blend old-world charm with contemporary amenities.

Our family-run hotel provides personalized service, authentic Sri Lankan hospitality, and a peaceful atmosphere perfect for relaxation or exploring the cultural triangle.
